Your Privacy

We care about your privacy. Price Tracker collects some information about your use of the add-on:

  • Product Pages: Price Tracker collects how often you visit saved product pages.
  • Product Data: Price Tracker collects data about the products you choose to track such as prices change frequency, size, and the accuracy of that information.
  • Interaction Data: Price Tracker collects information about how you use the feature such as how often you add, delete and interact with saved products, number of products you choose to track, and how you engage with messages and push notifications.

How do I opt-out of sending data to Mozilla?

Price Tracker uses Firefox's Telemetry system to collect data. You can opt-out of data collection by disabling Firefox Telemetry:

  1. Click the menu button and choose Preferences.
  2. Select the Privacy & Security panel.
  3. Scroll down to the Firefox Data Collection and Use section.
  4. Check or uncheck the box next to Allow Firefox to send technical and interaction data to Mozilla.
