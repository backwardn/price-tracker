Price Tracker is ending

Mozilla is ending official support for Firefox Price Tracker on September 30, 2019. Price Tracker will uninstall itself from Firefox in approximately 30 days. You may also choose to uninstall it now.

Price Tracker began as a proof-of-concept experiment in Firefox Test Pilot, our experimental feature development program. You can learn more about Price Tracker on the support page.

Test Pilot is making a comeback!

Firefox Test Pilot is coming back to life in a new form! Test Pilot users will help us refine promising new privacy and security features, for the future benefit of all Firefox users.

You can join the new Test Pilot early beta program by Joining Firefox. Sign in to your Firefox Account and subscribe to "New Product Testing" under your email preferences.
